Our Ingredients:

  • 100ml of Spring Water
  • 10g of Sugar
  • 20g of Matcha
  • 15g of Guarana

Steps to make the best energy drink:

  1. We insert the water into the mixer
  2. We add the Matcha to the mix
  3. We add the Guarana
  4. Finally we add in the sugar and mix it all up
